overview

NATURE'S BEAUTY AT YOUR DOORSTEP

Nestled in the heart of Mumbai, L&T's Elixir Reserve in Powai offers a serene sanctuary amidst the city's hustle and bustle. Picture expansive 2, 3, and 4 BHK residences, each a luxurious retreat surrounded by the tranquility of a lake, a majestic hillside, and a lush forest. The project's well-designed living spaces harmoniously blend with the natural beauty that serves as a constant backdrop.

Elixir Reserve goes beyond aesthetics, offering a comprehensive lifestyle package. Residents can look forward to a range of contemporary amenities, such as invigorating matches on the tennis court and refreshing swims in the pool, with possession expected by June 2026. The prime location of Powai ensures easy access to all of Mumbai's major transit hubs, placing you at the center of everything.
